Preparing Chester County for Personal Finance Education

Act 35 of 2023 requires all PA schools to provide a mandatory personal finance course (minimum 0.5 credits) for grades 9-12 starting in the 2026-27 school year. Explore free resources, upcoming events, and professional development opportunities below.
